About Fitness Tracker

Fitness Tracker is designed to help you stay consistent with your health goals. Track calories, monitor your weight, and visualize your progress — all in one place.

This project is built using Java Servlets, MySQL, and Bootstrap to deliver a simple and effective experience.

Our goal is to make fitness tracking easy, accessible, and motivating.
